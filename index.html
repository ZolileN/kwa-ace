A Legacy of
Entertainment.

Originally established in 2007 as Groova Lounge, the venue quickly set the standard for township nightlife in Cape Town. In 2018, the venue underwent a luxurious transformation, reopening as the multi-level Kwa Ace Lounge. This revamp introduced a state-of-the-art sound system, premier lounges, and a VVIP rooftop deck with a swimming pool.

Today, Kwa Ace is recognized nationally as a premier lifestyle and culinary landmark. From the high-tempo, DJ-fueled energy of the top floor to the welcoming, family-friendly tables of Ekitcheni on the ground floor, it is a location where memories are created, stories are shared, and taste reigns supreme.
